Payment QR assets
=================

Save the official payment QR images here before production launch:

- `esewa-qr.png`
- `khalti-qr.png`

The current paywall reads QR images from:

- `EXPO_PUBLIC_ESEWA_QR_URL`
- `EXPO_PUBLIC_KHALTI_QR_URL`

Use the real wallet app's downloaded QR image or a hosted image URL. Do not recreate a payment QR from the phone number; eSewa and Khalti QR codes contain provider-specific payment payloads.
